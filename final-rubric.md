# Final Deliverable Rubric — Product Launch Portfolio

**Course:** MKT2700 — Product Design & Development — Spring 2026
**Instructor:** Brad Scheller
**Weight:** 10% of Final Grade
**Note:** Individual grades are adjusted by peer evaluation (see Individual Adjustment section below).

---

## Scoring Scale

| Score | Label | Description |
|:-----:|-------|-------------|
| **4** | Exceptional | Exceeds expectations, professional quality |
| **3** | Strong | Meets all expectations with minor gaps |
| **2** | Adequate | Meets minimum requirements, notable weaknesses |
| **1** | Below expectations | Significant gaps or missing elements |
| **0** | Not submitted | — |

---

## Grading Dimensions

### 1. Capstone Report Quality (25%)

| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| **4** | Executive summary is compelling and self-contained. Journey narrative shows clear decision-making logic with specific examples. Market validation section includes data visualizations and cites persona data with precision. Go-to-market assessment is honest and detailed. Reflections are personal, specific, and demonstrate genuine learning. Professional formatting throughout. |
| **3** | All required sections present. Executive summary covers the basics. Journey narrative is coherent. Market validation data included but visualizations are basic. Go-to-market assessment present. Reflections show thought but are somewhat generic. |
| **2** | Some sections missing or thin. Executive summary is vague. Journey section reads as a chronological log rather than a narrative. Market data mentioned but not analyzed. Reflections are surface-level. |
| **1** | Report is incomplete, disorganized, or under 5 pages. Key sections missing. No data citations. Reflections are copy-pasted or perfunctory. |
| **0** | No report submitted. |

### 2. Final Presentation & Video (20%)

| Score | Criteria |
|:-----:|----------|
| **4** | Polished, well-rehearsed 10–15 minute presentation. All team members speak confidently. Clear narrative arc from problem to solution to validation. Data from Phase 3 woven into the story. Professional video quality (good audio, lighting, editing). Q&A section anticipates strong objections. Would be appropriate to show to real investors. |
| **3** | Good presentation covering all required segments. All members speak. Data referenced. Minor issues with timing, audio, or polish. Clear recommendation at the end. |
| **2** | Presentation covers basics but feels rushed or unrehearsed. Some segments missing or too long/short. Video quality is poor (bad audio, shaky camera). Not all members speak, or some clearly did not prepare. |
| **1** | Presentation is disorganized, reads from slides, or significantly over/under time. Missing major segments. Video is a raw screen recording with no editing. |
| **0** | No video submitted. |

#### For Software Applications

|:-----:|----------|

| **2** | Demo shows partial functionality. Key features missing or broken. Feels like a work-in-progress. |
| **1** | Screenshots only, no working demo. OR demo crashes or clearly doesn't work. |
| **0** | No demo submitted. |

|:-----:|----------|

| **2** | Limited photos. No context shots. Spec sheet is vague. |
| **1** | One or two low-quality images. No spec sheet. |

| Score | Criteria |

| **3** | Brand materials present. Service flow or mockups included. Business model described. |

## Grade Calculation

### Team Base Score

The team base score is the weighted total of all six dimensions:

```
Team Base Score = (Capstone × 0.25)
               + (Presentation × 0.20)
               + (Demo × 0.15)
               + (Validation × 0.15)
               + (Completeness × 0.10)
               + (Individual × 0.15)
```

**Percentage** = (Weighted Score / 4.0) × 100

#### Example

| Dimension | Score | Weight | Weighted |
|-----------|:-----:|:------:|:--------:|
| Capstone Report | 3 | 0.25 | 0.75 |
| Presentation & Video | 4 | 0.20 | 0.80 |
| Product Demo | 3 | 0.15 | 0.45 |
| Market Validation | 3 | 0.15 | 0.45 |
| Portfolio Completeness | 4 | 0.10 | 0.40 |
| Individual Contribution | 3 | 0.15 | 0.45 |
| **Total** | | | **3.30** |

Percentage = (3.30 / 4.0) × 100 = **82.5%** (B−)

---

### Individual Adjustment

The team base score is adjusted per individual based on peer evaluation:

| Average Peer Score | Adjustment |
|:------------------:|------------|
| 4.0–5.0 | No adjustment (up to +5% bonus for truly exceptional contributors) |
| 3.0–3.9 | No adjustment |
| 2.0–2.9 | −10% penalty |
| 1.0–1.9 | −25% penalty |
| Below 1.0 | −50% penalty (or individual meeting with instructor) |
